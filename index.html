scRNA-seq Analysis of the Pancreatic Cancer Tumor Microenvironment

Single-cell RNA-seq analysis of the PDAC tumor microenvironment using GEO dataset GSE155698. Performed QC, clustering, canonical marker annotation, and per-patient differential expression analysis in R using Seurat.

Key finding: Macrophages form the dominant immune population consistent with immunosuppressive TME characteristics in PDAC. Distinct CAF/tumor compartments and inter-patient tumor heterogeneity confirm why PDAC is treatment-resistant.

CMS Medicare Provider Billing Analysis

SQL-driven analysis of CMS Medicare Part B provider data. Built a SQLite database from 9M+ records and used multi-table joins, window functions, and subqueries to surface payment patterns across specialties and geographies.
