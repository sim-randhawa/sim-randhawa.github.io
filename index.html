TCGA PAAD Survival Analysis — Pancreatic Cancer Clinical Outcomes

End-to-end clinical survival analysis of pancreatic adenocarcinoma patients using TCGA-PAAD data. Performed data cleaning in Python, Kaplan-Meier survival estimation, log-rank testing by stage and gender, and multivariate Cox proportional hazards regression in R.
